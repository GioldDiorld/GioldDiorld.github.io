About

Hi everyone, it’s GD here.

I am trying to write a blog in English, not because I love English or I like showing off my poor English.

I didn’t write blogs in the last two years of college because I found too many beautiful blogs written by others, which made me regard it as meaningless to write a blog with the same content but less attraction. Therefore, my first reason is that I want to write something different. It’s clear that it will be hard to write differently in terms of content, so I just thought, “Maybe I’ll write differently in terms of language.”

Nevertheless, the biggest reason is that I have been reminded that blogs, being a kind of note, are something that you try to arrange on your own and improve at the same time.

Simultaneously, after the college entrance examination, I lost all my goals and I tried to do something that can make me feel meaningful and happy. I hope blogging will be the last thing I try, meaning that it will become something that I can really persist in instead of giving up.

To be honest, despite being a student majoring in cybersecurity, I am not so interested in programming or debugging. In fact, my favorite thing to do is living without pressure and playing games that do not cost too much energy (like galgames).

I’m about to be in my 20s this June, and I always feel quite nervous and scared of being someone useless, although I find it very possible to become the truth in despair.

I know that I’m already too old to be somebody, but I’m still so young to be nobody.

It’s never too late to change.

This is where I start my blog.

GioldDiorld
